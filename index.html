Sound Effects Records, presents “Garden Electric”, the debut album by, Greece’s best-kept prog-rock secret, Jupiter Fungus! Essentially a duo, with the aid of select studio musicians, Jupiter Fungus combine captivating themes with meticulous arrangements and passionate performances, not unlike their heroes. Their holistic approach, incl. their breathtaking artwork, adds to the legacy of “Garden Electric”, making for one of the best new albums of the genre. “Garden Electric” shall be out on limited gatefold vinyl, CD and digital on November 1st, 2024.
